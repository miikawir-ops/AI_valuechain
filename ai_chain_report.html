About this dashboard

A daily signal dashboard tracking the AI supply chain — from power plants to software — identifying where investment opportunities and risks are building.

🔴 Hot / Bottleneck
Current dominant constraint. Supply cannot meet demand. Highest conviction signal.
🟠 Emerging
Building momentum. Becoming the next bottleneck, or price running ahead of fundamentals.
🟢 Neutral / Healthy
Stable. No constraint pressure. Not a concern, not an urgent opportunity.
🔵 Cooling
Previously hot layer. Constraint easing, growth decelerating.
Warning flags

⚡ Narrative ahead of fundamentals — Investment story exists in news but not yet confirmed in quarterly earnings. Wait for financial confirmation.

🟠 Orange card — Stock price has moved significantly more than revenue growth justifies. Market may have already priced in the good news.

What the colors mean
Red — current bottleneck
All three signal categories agree. This layer is the dominant constraint in the AI supply chain right now.
Orange — emerging or hype warning
Building momentum toward a bottleneck, or price action is outrunning the fundamentals.
Green — neutral / healthy
Stable activity, no constraint pressure. Functioning normally.
Blue — cooling
Growth acceleration declining. A previously hot layer easing off.
Note on AI Security layer: Security companies (CRWD, PANW) are steady compounders growing at 10–25% annually — not hardware bottlenecks. A Blue/Green Security layer is normal and healthy. Watch for Orange/Red only during major breach cycles or regulatory shifts.
